# 05 — Editorial Guidelines

## Content Pillars (Content Diet)

| Pillar | % | Cadence | Format |
|--------|---|---------|--------|
| **Strategy Thinking** | 35% | 2x/week | Articles, threads, videos |
| **Real Stories** | 35% | 2x/week | Case studies, failures, wins |
| **Tactics & Playbooks** | 30% | 1.5x/week | Step-by-step, checklists, tools |

**Total:** 5.5 pieces/week (distribution agnostic)

---

## Editorial Principles

### ✅ DO
- Lead with data, not opinion ("50% of creators fail at monetization because...")
- Show the journey, not just the result ("Here's how we tested 3 positioning angles")
- Educate, then sell ("Here's the framework. Applied it? Come build with us.")
- Acknowledge reality ("This works IF you actually implement")
- Share what doesn't work ("We tried sponsorships. Here's why they failed for us")

### ❌ DON'T
- Promise unrealistic results ("Make $100k in 90 days" — cap)
- Hide the hard work ("It's easy!" — lie)
- Gatekeep knowledge ("This is only for paid members" — kills trust)
- Follow trends blindly ("Everyone's doing TikTok so..." — irrelevant)
- Fake social proof ("1M+ creators trust us" — verify, don't assume)

## Key Messages (Repetition)

Use these 3 messages consistently across all content:

### Message 1: Positioning is Your Moat
"Your personal brand is your business moat. Positioning beats audience size every time."
- Use in: Strategy content, value prop, conversations
- Frequency: 1-2x/week minimum

### Message 2: Monetization is a Playbook
"There are 7 proven monetization playbooks for creators. Master 2-3, not all 7."
- Use in: Product education, strategy sessions
- Frequency: 1x/week minimum

### Message 3: Systems Enable Scaling
"Burn-out is not a badge. Build systems, not heroes. Scale sustainably."
- Use in: Wellbeing content, framework sharing
- Frequency: 1x/week minimum

## Content Quality Standards

### Research & Accuracy
- All stats backed by source (link it)
- Case studies use real examples (anonymize if needed)
- Frameworks tested before publishing

### Clarity & Accessibility  
- Jargon explained on first use
- Complex ideas broken into 3-5 steps
- Always include a TL;DR (take-home message)

### Actionability
- Every piece should have: Hook → Insight → Action
- Action is specific ("Do this by Friday")
- Never leave readers thinking "ok cool, now what?"

## Content Formats & Templates

### 📝 Format 1: Strategy Article (1,200-1,500 words)
```
Title: "[Action] + [Outcome] = [Unexpected Result]"
Example: "Positioning Over Growth = 3x Revenue in Same Audience"

Structure:
1. Hook (personal story, 150 words)
2. Problem (what most creators get wrong, 200 words)
3. Framework (3 parts, 600 words)
4. Implementation (step-by-step, 300 words)
5. Mistakes (what to avoid, 200 words)
6. CTA (what's next, 50 words)
```

### 📸 Format 2: Story Case Study (500-800 words)
```
Title: "[Creator Name] Went From [Before] to [After]"

Structure:
1. Intro (who, context, 100 words)
2. Challenge (their specific problem, 150 words)
3. Solution (what they did differently, 200 words)
4. Results (numbers, 100 words)
5. Lessons (3 key takeaways, 200 words)
```

### ✅ Format 3: Playbook Checklist (300-500 words)
```
Title: "[Outcome] Checklist: [#] Steps to [Result]"

Structure:
1. Intro (why this matters, 100 words)
2. Checklist (5-10 items with explanations, 300 words)
3. Common mistakes (3-5 gotchas, 100 words)
4. Next step (where to go after, 50 words)
```

## Channel-Specific Tweaks

### LinkedIn (B2B, Professionals)
- Tone: 80% authority, 20% personality
- Focus: Strategy, positioning, business thinking
- Format: Long articles, threads, video storytelling
- Frequency: 3-4x/week

### Twitter/X (Real-time, Community)
- Tone: 60% personality, 40% insight  
- Focus: Hot takes, quick tactics, reactions
- Format: Threads, replies, quick shares
- Frequency: 5-7x/week

### YouTube (Authority, In-depth)
- Tone: 70% teaching, 30% personality
- Focus: Deep dives, case studies, frameworks
- Format: 15-45min videos, series
- Frequency: 1-2x/week

### Newsletter (Owned, Relationship)
- Tone: 100% authentic (most personal channel)
- Focus: Weekly reflections, curated insights, community
- Format: Written, conversational, personal
- Frequency: 1x/week (consistent day)
