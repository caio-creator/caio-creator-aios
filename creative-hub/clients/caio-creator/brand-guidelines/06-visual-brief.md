# 06 — Visual Brand Brief

## Design System at a Glance

### Color Palette
```
Primary: #2563EB (Criação Azul) — Trust, strategy, knowledge
Secondary: #F97316 (Energia Laranja) — Energy, creativity, call-to-action  
Neutral: #0A0A0B to #FAFAFA (Dark first theme)
Accent Success: #10B981
Accent Warning: #F59E0B
Accent Error: #EF4444
```

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Inter 600-900 weights, 48px-64px
- **Body:** Inter 400-600 weights, 14px-18px
- **Accessibility:** Min contrast ratio 4.5:1

### Layout Principles
- **Spacing:** 8px grid (4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48px)
- **Border Radius:** 4px (UI), 8px (cards), 12px (containers)
- **Shadows:** Minimal, only on hover/active states

### Logo Usage
- **Minimum size:** 40px (digital), 1cm (print)
- **Clear space:** 20px minimum padding
- **Backgrounds:** High contrast always
- **Colors:** Primary blue or white only

---

## Design Applications

### Digital
✅ **Website:** Dark background with blue highlights, minimal animations
✅ **Email:** Simple layout, brand colors on CTAs only
✅ **Social:** Square/vertical formats, bold imagery + clean text
✅ **App:** Dark theme default, accessibility-first

### Print  
✅ **Business Card:** Vertical layout, blue gradient, minimal text
✅ **Stationary:** Blue accent stripe, consistent logo placement
✅ **Brochures:** Folded standard, text-heavy + image balance
✅ **Presentations:** Dark title slides, white content slides

---

## Forbidden Practices
❌ Distort or rotate logo
❌ Change primary colors
❌ Use outdated logo versions
❌ Place logo on complex backgrounds
❌ Apply effects (shadows, glows, etc.)
❌ Use more than 2 secondary fonts
