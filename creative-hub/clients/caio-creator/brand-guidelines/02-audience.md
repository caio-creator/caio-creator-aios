# 02 — Target Audience

## Persona 1: The Ambitious Creator
**🎯 Primary Audience**
- Age: 22-32 | Location: Brasil/LA | Income: R$2k-8k/mês
- Platform: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube (building phase)
- Pain: "I have an audience but I'm not making money"
- Desire: Real revenue, sustainable business, not selling out
- Decision driver: Proof (case studies, implementable tactics)

**How to reach:** YouTube tutorials, TikTok breakdowns, email community

## Persona 2: The Business-Minded Entrepreneur
**💼 Secondary Audience**
- Age: 28-42 | Location: Brasil/Global | Income: R$8k-30k/mês
- Platform: LinkedIn, podcasts, private communities
- Pain: "I have a brand but I'm not leveraging it strategically"
- Desire: Premium positioning, higher prices, strategic partnerships
- Decision driver: ROI, exclusive access, mentorship

**How to reach:** LinkedIn articles, masterclasses, 1:1 consulting

## Persona 3: The Creator at Crossroads
**🔄 Tertiary Audience**
- Age: 25-35 | Location: Brasil/Global | Income: Variable
- Platform: Private communities, podcasts, direct messages
- Pain: "I'm burned out. Should I keep going or pivot?"
- Desire: Permission to change + strategic roadmap
- Decision driver: Authentic insights, real talk, community support

**How to reach:** Honest storytelling, community Discord, open conversations
