# 07 — Digital Guidelines

## Web Presence Standards

### Website
- **Theme:** Dark first (dark mode default)
- **Speed:** Core Web Vitals all green
- **Accessibility:** WCAG AA minimum
- **Mobile:** Touch-friendly, min 44px targets
- **CTA colors:** Blue primary, orange secondary
- **Fonts:** Inter only (no custom fonts per section)

### Email
- **Width:** 600px max (mobile-optimized)
- **Colors:** Use blue/orange sparingly
- **Links:** Underlined, hover state clear
- **Footer:** Logo small (40px), social links optional
- **Template:** Company-safe, no scripts

### Social Media

**Instagram/Facebook**
- Format: Square (1080x1080), Vertical (1080x1350)
- Text: Max 125 characters on image (readable)
- Colors: Leverage brand palette in designs
- Posting: 5-6x/week consistent schedule

**LinkedIn**
- Format: Horizontal (1200x627) or vertical (1200x1500)
- Text: Professional tone (70% authority, 30% personality)
- Links: Always included in first comment
- Posting: 3-4x/week, consistent times

**Twitter/X**
- Format: Text-first, 280 characters
- Threads: Use for longer thoughts
- Replies: Engage with community 2-3x/day
- Posting: 5-7x/week, organic flow

### Mobile App (if applicable)
- **Design:** Dark theme, blue accents
- **Navigation:** Bottom tab, max 4 primary
- **Typography:** Larger fonts (16px+ body)
- **Buttons:** Min 44px height/width
- **Images:** Optimized for mobile (compressed)

---

## Content Distribution Checklist

Before publishing any content:

- ✅ Headline is benefit-focused ("Your X gets Y result")
- ✅ Image/visual included (video thumbnail or hero image)
- ✅ Link included (to full article, free resource, or CTA)
- ✅ Formatting: Short paragraphs, bold key phrases
- ✅ Call-to-action: "Reply," "Comment," "Share," "Save"
- ✅ Brand colors applied appropriately
- ✅ Proofread 2x for typos/clarity
- ✅ Accessibility: Alt text for images, captions for video
